## Why this book?

In professional development and large team projects, version control is a must. Git is powerful, but its many features can feel overwhelming at first. This guide is your go-to introduction, covering both beginner and advanced commands, so you feel confident initializing a repo, managing updates, and resolving conflicts.

The difference here: instead of static screenshots, the tricky concepts come with **interactive animations** you can step through at your own pace.
